About Us

Fun-e-Learning Corporation is a locally SEC registered corporation created to bring out the best from Filipino learners.
Taking advantages of the expertise and experience of the people of Fun-e-Learning, we created educational materials for the Filipino learners.

MISSION / VISION

The mission of Fun-e-Learning is to provide the Filipino learners with video materials useful to their growth and applicable to their daily lives.

The vision of Fun-e-Learning is to have a significant part in making the Filipinos among the top learners in the world.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES PAGE

Viducation

Viducation is a digital library with a purpose of bringing out the best in Filipino learners. Viducation comes in several groups, namely:

  • Viducation Short Stories. These are interesting and short stories designed to build the characters of the learners. The lessons are expected to give the learners a better appreciation of themselves and others.
  • Viducation Soft Skill. These are video materials created to enhance the personality of the learners.
  • Viducation Call Center. In 2024, 40% of grade 12 learners will not proceed to college upon graduation. To address this concern, we created learning tools designed to prepare these students be qualified as BPO employees. More so, we made arrangement with the leading BPO companies to consider them for employment after they completed Senior High School.

Fun-e-ducation

Fun-e-ducation are educational face-to-face seminars structured to improve important skills. Some among the subjects of this program are confidence building, job interview tips, effective resume preparation, and many more.

Fun-e-Xam

Fun-e-Xam. These are online assessments created to measure the amount of learnings the learners have gathered.

PRODUCT LINES AND SERVICES

Taking advantage of our vast experience and impressive expertise, Fun-e-Learning Corporation offers the following solutions:

  1. Viducation Soft Skills. These are set of video contents that were created enhance the personality and characters of the learners. Some among the materials that we created are Confidence Building, Developing Listening Skills, Developing Pleasing Personality and many more.
  2. Viducation Short Stories. These are array of short stories presented in digital animation format. These stories are coupled with practical and important lessons. One of the objectives of these materials is to allow the Filipino learners deeply appreciate their environments.
  3. Viducation Academic. On the premise that most Filipino learners are visual learners, and text books getting to be more and more costly, we created visual materials that are more appealing that offers the same content as the text books. Equally important, the learners may enjoy the learning for FREE.
  4. Fun-e-Valuation. This is our attempt to allow the schools manage and handle the learners. We accomplish this objective by providing series of online Psychological instruments together with the outcome. These tools are provided for FREE.
